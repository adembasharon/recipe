Chicken stew

The chicken is delicacy all across Kenya.Here's how to prepare it

Ingridients

  • kienyeji chicken
  • Garlic
  • ginger
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • salt to test
  • C.oil

Procedure

  1. boil the chicken with salt,garlic till tender
  2. In pan heat C.oil till hot
  3. Add your boilled chicken in batches.fry till brown
  4. In a sufuria fry onion till it starts browning
  5. Add in your chicken and star
  6. Add blended tomatoes and cover the sufuria
  7. cook under lo heat till tomatoes are fully cooked
  8. Add litle water.Let it simmer for some minutes
  9. carnish with dhania
